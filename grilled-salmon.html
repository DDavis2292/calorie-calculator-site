Grilled Salmon Recipe

Description

A beautifully balanced, restaurant-quality entrée featuring a perfectly grilled salmon fillet with crisp, caramelized grill marks and a tender, flaky interior. The salmon is finished with a fresh lemon slice and a rich balsamic glaze that adds a subtle sweetness and acidity. It rests on a warm bed of red and white quinoa, offering a nutty texture and wholesome depth, while a side of lightly roasted asparagus provides a fresh, vibrant contrast. Served on modern dishware, this dish is both elegant and nourishing—ideal for a refined weeknight dinner or an impressive plated meal.

Ingredients

For the Salmon:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh cracked black pepper
  • 1 lemon (sliced into rounds)

For the Quinoa:

  • 1 cup red and white quinoa (mixed)
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • Pinch of salt

For the Asparagus:

  • 1 bunch asparagus (trimmed)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Instructions

1. Cook the Quinoa:

  • Rinse quinoa under cold water.
  • In a saucepan, bring quinoa, water (or broth) and salt toa boil.
  • Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed
  • Fluff with a fork and set aside.

2. Roast the Asparagus:

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit.
  • Toss asparagus with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Roast for 10-12 minutes until tender with slight crispness.

3. Grill the Salmon:

  • Preheat grill pan or outdoor grill to medium-high heat.
  • Brush salmon with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  • Grill skin-side down for 4-5 minutes, then flip and cook another 3-4 minutes until cooked through and flaky

4. Assemble the Dish:

  • Spoon a bed of quinoa onto the plate.
  • Place grilled salmon on top.
  • Add a lemon slice over the salmon
  • Lay the asparagus on the the side./li>
  • Drizzle balsamic glaze over the salmon, quinoa, and asparagus.

ENJOY!
