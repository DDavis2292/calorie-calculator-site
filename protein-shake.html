Strawberry Peanut Butter Cup Protein Shake

Description

A creamy, chocolatey protein shake with the perfect balance of sweet strawberries and rich peanut butter. It tastes like a peanut butter cup with a fresh, fruity twist—while still being packed with protein, healthy fats, and natural ingredients. Great for post-workout or a filling breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or milk of choice)
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon natural peanut butter (no added sugar)
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ frozen banana (for creaminess)
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds (optional, for added fiber)
  • Ice (optional, for thicker texture)

Instructions

1. Add almond milk to a blender first.

2. Add protein powder, frozen strawberries, peanut butter, cocoa powder, and banana.

3. Blend until smooth and creamy.

4. Add ice if you want it thicker, then blend again.

5. Taste and adjust (more peanut butter for richness, more strawberries for brightness).

Optional Upgrades

  • Add a few dark chocolate chips for texture
  • Swap banana for frozen cauliflower (keeps it creamy, lowers sugar)
  • Add spinach (you won’t taste it, but boosts nutrients)
  • Use powdered peanut butter to reduce calories

Nutrition Highlights

  • High protein
  • Healthy fats
  • Naturally sweetened
  • Good source of fiber
