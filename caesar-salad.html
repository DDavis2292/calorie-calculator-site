Caesar Salad Recipe

Description

A modern take on a classic Caesar salad, this dish features crisp romaine lettuce tossed in a creamy, savory Caesar dressing and topped with perfectly grilled, sliced chicken breast. Finished with freshly shaved Parmesan cheese, crunchy golden croutons, and a generous sprinkle of cracked black pepper, each bite delivers a balance of texture and bold flavor. Delicately placed anchovies on the side add a traditional, briny depth for those who appreciate the authentic Caesar experience. Presented in sleek, contemporary dishware, this salad is both elevated and satisfying.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 large head romaine lettuce (chopped)
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup shaved Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh cracked black pepper

For the Chicken (Optional):

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

For Garnish:

  • Lemon wedge (for garnish)
  • While anchovy fillets (for plating)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Chicken:

  • Preheat grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Rub chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Cook for 5-6 minutes per side until fully cooked (internal temp 165 degrees fahrenheit).
  • Let rest, then slice into even strips.

2. Make the Caesar Dressing:

  • In a bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, garlic, and anchovies.
  • Stir in grated Parmesan and olive oil
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust consistency if needed with a small splash of water.

3. Assemble the Salad:

  • In a large bowl, toss romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
  • Add croutons and gently mix.

4. Plate the Dish:

  • Transfer salad to serving bowl or plate.
  • Arrange sliced grilled chicken on top.
  • Sprinkle with shaved Parmesan and cracked black pepper.
  • Add a few anchovy fillets on the side for garnish.
  • Squeeze a lemon wedge over the salad (avoid letting the seeds get into the salad).
